The Best Movies According to Percy

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The way of the Dragon

This is my favourite film. Is a 1972 Hong Kong martial arts action comedy film co-produced and directed by Bruce Lee.

Armour of God

"Armour of God" is a 1986 Hong Kong action-comedy written and directed by Jackie Chan, who also starred in the film in the lead role.

Pretty Woman

The film's title Pretty Woman is based on the 1964 song "Oh, Pretty Woman" by Roy Orbison.

Super beautiful film. Really artistic.
